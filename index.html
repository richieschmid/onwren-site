How it works

Three steps. Zero reading required.

Save anything from the web, get a clear spoken summary, listen whenever suits you.

01

Save the article

Click the Wren extension on any article, blog post, or research paper. Or paste a URL directly into the app. Wren captures the full text instantly.

02

Wren summarizes it

Our AI reads the article and generates a clear, spoken summary — a few minutes for standard articles, up to 20 minutes for long-form research. No fluff, no filler.

03

Listen anywhere

Open Wren on your phone and your queue is ready. Listen on your commute, at the gym, while cooking — whenever reading isn't an option.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for people who take their reading seriously.

Chrome extension

Save any article you're reading with one click. Works on news sites, blogs, journals, and most paywalled content you're already subscribed to.

Mobile-first app

Add Wren to your home screen for a native app feel. Queue syncs across all your devices — save on desktop, listen on your phone.

PDF support
