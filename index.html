How it works

Three steps. Zero reading required.

Save anything from the web, get a clear spoken summary, listen whenever suits you.

01

Save the article

Click the Wren extension on any article, blog post, or research paper. Or paste a URL directly into the app. Wren captures the full text instantly.

02

Wren summarizes it

Wren generates a clear, spoken summary — a few minutes for standard articles, up to 10 minutes for long-form research. No fluff, no filler.

03

Listen anywhere

Open Wren on your phone and your queue is ready. Listen on your commute, at the gym, while cooking — whenever reading isn't an option.
